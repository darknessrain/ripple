Scenario

daily life, from morning to night

MORNING

To start a day, Ripple LIGHT will turn on automatically to wake you up in the morning.

Ripple PLUG already turn on the connected electric kettle to boil water for you to make coffee.

LEAVE

Once you leave home, Ripple PLUG turn off connected appliances to save power for reducing your bill payment.

Ripple SHOCK detects shock and glass breakage, you’ll get notification if someone is trying to break window.

Ripple SMOKE detects smoke and heat, it sends alarm and also notification.

HOME

Ripple DOOR monitor your home entrance, it sends you notification when door is open or you can check status on APP anytime.

When door is open, Ripple DOOR triggers Ripple LIGHT or Ripple PLUG to turn on, your hallway is bright to welcome you home.

GOOD NIGHT

When it’s time to bed, Ripple PLUG will turn off connected appliance and Ripple LIGHT will dim automatically, just set the schedule in APP.

If it gets hot, Ripple WEATHER will tell PLUG to turn on the connected fan, you don’t need to wake up in the middle of night.
